Beat Map™ Linguistic Execution Technology

Zadeo Inc. develops and deploys Beat Map™ linguistic execution technology, a structured language-to-action translation layer that supports the translation of human-authored directives into measurable execution cues.

Beat Map technology encodes timing, emphasis, sequencing, and priority signals directly into language artifacts, enabling consistent interpretation across AI systems, human operators, and hybrid workflows. This capability supports precise instruction delivery, auditability, and reduced ambiguity in high-stakes operational contexts.
